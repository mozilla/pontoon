Additional documentation

For in-depth documentation on Mozilla localization, see our Documentation for Mozilla localizers which includes in depth explanations on topics such as:

  • Community roles
  • Specific Mozilla product localization (such as Firefox, Firefox for Android, etc.)
  • Tools such as Pontoon and Fluent (the advanced translation format used by Mozilla)

Pontoon Add-on

You can use the Pontoon Add-on for Firefox or Chrome to be instantly notified of any Pontoon notifications right in your browser, as well as some helpful tools to help with proofreading, bug reporting, and fixing.

Mozilla community participation guidelines

As an active member of our community, we’d like you to be aware of the Mozilla community participation guidelines to ensure the wellbeing of all members of the community.

Email settings

Finally, if you’re interested in the latest updates, announcements about new Pontoon features, invitations to contributor events and more, be sure to enable “News and updates“ in your settings so you can be the first to get notified.
